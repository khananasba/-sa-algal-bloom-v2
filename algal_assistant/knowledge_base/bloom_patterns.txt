Important note on data priority:
Ground truth water sampling is the most accurate data source.
SA Government field scientists collect water samples and count Karenia
cells under microscope giving exact cell counts in cells per litre.
This platform has 509 ground truth readings from 512 SA beach sites
collected since September 2025.
Satellite SFABI readings support but cannot replace ground truth.
When ground truth data is available always lead with cell count first.

Seasonal bloom patterns in South Australia:
Blooms peak October to November in Gulf St Vincent near Adelaide.
Spencer Gulf blooms peak September to November near Port Lincoln.
Karenia requires warm sea surface temperature above 18 degrees Celsius.
Calm low wind conditions favour bloom formation and persistence.
Blooms can spread from Spencer Gulf southward via ocean currents.
Historically safest months for coastal excursions: April to August.
Historically highest risk months: October November December January.
Metropolitan Adelaide coast is high risk October to December.
Eyre Peninsula Spencer Gulf is high risk September to November.
