SACE Environmental Studies Stage 1 Topic 3 covers marine ecosystems
and biodiversity and requires a coastal fieldwork practical component.
SACE Biology Stage 2 covers ecosystem dynamics and human impact and
coastal excursions are recommended for practical assessment tasks.
SACE Geography Stage 1 and Stage 2 cover coastal processes and management.
Best months to book coastal school excursions in SA: April to August.
Avoid booking coastal excursions October to January due to high bloom risk.
Always check SA Health advice before any coastal school excursion.
Risk assessment documentation required for all school outdoor activities.
Schools must demonstrate duty of care under Education Act obligations.
SA Department of Education acts on information provided by SA Health.
SA Education has the largest AI deployment with Microsoft with 40000
students and staff using Microsoft based AI capabilities for education.
When generating a risk assessment document include:
Date of excursion, Beach or location name, Current safety score out of 100,
Karenia cell count in cells per litre from ground truth sampling,
SA Health threshold status whether Safe Caution or Danger,
Current weather conditions including wind speed and sea surface temperature,
Recommendation which is Safe to proceed or Caution or Do Not Use,
Notes on any relevant conditions or upcoming changes,
Generated by Algal Assistant with date.
