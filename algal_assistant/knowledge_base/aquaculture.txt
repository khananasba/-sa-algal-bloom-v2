SA Southern Bluefin Tuna industry annual value: 180 million AUD.
Approximately 30 tuna farms operating in Spencer Gulf near Port Lincoln.
Karenia mortality threshold for Southern Bluefin Tuna: 10000 cells/L.
Main farm clusters: Port Lincoln, Boston Bay, Proper Bay, Coffin Bay.
Fish mortality occurs within 24 to 48 hours of sustained bloom exposure.
72 hour advance warning gives operators time to reduce feeding and prepare.
PIRSA is the regulatory authority for SA aquaculture operations.
Economic loss from a major bloom event can reach tens of millions AUD.
Early detection and 72hr forecasting is critical for industry survival.
